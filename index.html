A desktop application that combines Messenger and Instagram into one single UI

Features

Dark Mode

Easily toggle between Dark or Light theme from Messengram's menu (File > Preferences > Toggle Dark Mode) or by pressing Ctrl/Cmd + D on your keyboard.

Toggle Views

Want to chat on Messenger? Or maybe view some IG stories? You can easily choose the platform that you want to focus by navigating to File > Preferences and selecting one of the following options:

  • - Messenger & Instagram (Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + 1)
  • - Messenger only (Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + 2)
  • - Instagram only (Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + 3)

Cross platform

Enjoy Messengram whether you're a Windows, MacOS or Linux user. It works seamlessly on all major Operating Systems!
